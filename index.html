Our Mission

We seek to end domestic violence and empower survivors to lie healthy, independent lives free from violence and abuse. This site exists to aid past and current victims who have suffered from this traumatizing situation in the healing process. We are aware of the fact that not everyone is willing to share these issues with others in real life, so we decided to create a safe area where users may give advice and share their stories.No one is judged here, and we address your problems like we would our own.
